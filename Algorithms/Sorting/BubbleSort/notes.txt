What is BubbleSort?
    A sorting algorithm that allows users to sort given data by placing the largest elements at the correct spot on each itteration
    It looks at every pair or elements next to each other and compares them until the data is sorted
    It uses two for loops to be executed
Why use BubbleSort?
    When data is small or is nearly sorted
Time Complexity is O(n^2)
Space Complexity is O(1)
